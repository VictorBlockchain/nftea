Honey Pot

The honey pot is a community "bank". Your NFT earns tokens from the Honey Pot, each time community members add Honey to your NFT.

Adding honey is our version of the like button.

Brew Pot

Single mint NFT's each have a Brew Pot, think of it as a vault you can't open until a certain date.

It's like Tea you can't drink until it's finished brewing.

The owner of the NFT can withdraw the SatoShitCoin (or other shitcoin) tokens in the brew pot after it's brew date.
